MS Dhoni - The Legend

Early Life

MS Dhoni was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Bihar (now Jharkhand), India. He was a goalkeeper for his school's football team and played badminton as well. He was selected for district-level cricket matches, where his exceptional wicketkeeping skills were noticed.

Career

MS Dhoni made his ODI debut for India against Bangladesh on December 23, 2004. He was appointed as the captain of the Indian cricket team in 2007, leading India to victory in the T20 World Cup in the same year. Under his captaincy, India won the 2011 Cricket World Cup and the 2013 Champions Trophy.

Achievements

MS Dhoni is the only captain to win all three ICC trophies (T20 World Cup, 50-over World Cup, and Champions Trophy). He has the most number of stumpings (123) in ODI cricket. He is also the first wicketkeeper to complete 100 stumpings in ODI cricket.
